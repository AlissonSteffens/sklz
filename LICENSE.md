## March Rain, Open Code

Look how free a thing it is,
how loose, how given —
this code that flows
from me to you, and from you
to whoever comes after.

Use it, change it,
carry it softly
into your own work,
the way a breeze carries a seed
not knowing where it will land.

Only remember to say
where this water came from —
every source has a name,
and the name is so little,
but it's what remains.

The rest passes,
like a summer afternoon passes,
like love passes
that we hold
without holding.
